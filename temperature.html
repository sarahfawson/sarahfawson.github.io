Summary: Latitude vs. Temperature



Although this looks like a visualization of Florida during Spring Break, it is actually a plot of the trend and correlation between temperature and latitude. As expected, the temperature rises as you get closer to the equator. The colors on this graph do not represent anything in particular, I was just experimenting with using different colors and color families in matplotlib.
