Hello there!

MY NAME IS SARAH FAWSON. LET ME TELL YOU A BIT ABOUT MYSELF.

I am an aspiring Data Journalist with a background in Supply Chain Management. I received a BA in Supply Chain Management from the University of Oklahoma then gained experience in strategic operations planning, data analysis and reporting in both a global chemical company and a marketing agency. I am now taking an intensive 24-week bootcamp course in Data Analytics & Visualization from George Washington University, where I am learning Javascript, D3, Python, R, HTML/CSS, SQL, Git/Github and Tableau. I will finish this program in May 2019.

As a data journalist, my goal is to use data analysis and design to translate ambiguous data into relatable stories to help people understand topics and make informed decisions. I've seen the power of data visualization firsthand as I've used it to help people understand an autoimmune disease that I have. It has also helped me effectively educate others about the treatment and cost of this life-long disease. This experience has motivated me to change career paths entirely into data visualization and data journalism. These personal experiences, my knowledge of data analytics, experience in design and natural curiosity make me a great fit for this path, and SO excited to dig my heels in deeper and really take off with it.

When I'm not working, I'm probably illustrating, painting, rock climbing with my husband, or playing with my adorable dog Pablo.
