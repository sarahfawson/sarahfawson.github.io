When is Time Served?

SARAH FAWSON | MICA INVIZ THESIS

The justice system exists to protect Americans and provide fair treatment and rehabilitation to those convicted of a crime. There are major disparities in the expressed goals of the justice system and what is happening in reality, and the results are striking. I would like to create a comprehensive analysis of the burden of criminal disenfranchisement in America and how time is never truly served, but prolonged for the rest of someoneâ€™s life through restrictions on political rights, employment, and opportunities, all of which leads to higher rates of racial discrimination and recidivism.
