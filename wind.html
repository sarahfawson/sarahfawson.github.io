Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed



Through this analysis I found that there is no noticeable relationship between latitude and wind speed. It does not get windier or less windy, the closer you travel to the equator. The colors on this graph do not represent anything in particular, I was just experimenting with using different colors and color families in matplotlib.
